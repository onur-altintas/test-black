INFORMATION SHEET

           

The Effect of Decision Support Systems on Complex Decision-Making Processes

 

Principal Investigator – Onur Altintas,

PhD Candidate in Information Systems at Boston University

 

Co-Investigator – Prof. Abraham Seidmann,

The Everett W. Lord Distinguished Faculty Scholar of Information Systems at Boston University

 

 

This form describes a research study that is being conducted by Onur Altintas, a PhD Candidate from Boston University and his Faculty Advisor, Professor Abraham Seidmann. Onur Altintas can be reached at onura@bu.edu and Professor Abraham Seidmann can be reached at avis@bu.edu.    

The purpose of this study is to investigate the effect of decision support systems on decision processes in the newsvendor setting.

We are asking you to take part in this study because you have completed at least one statistics course in college.

If you decide to take part in this study, you will be asked to take part in the business simulation that will take about 30 minutes. We estimate that approximately 30 people will take part in this study.

There is no physical risk in this study. You might get tired during the tasks. You can rest at any time. Some of the survey questions may be upsetting or make you feel uncomfortable. You can skip any of the questions you do not want to answer. We will not collect and store any directly identifiable information about you with your survey responses. All of the information we collect will be stored on an encrypted hard disk and/or an encrypted cloud drive. Only the members of the research team will have access to it.

Loss of Confidentiality

The main risk of allowing us to use and store your information for research is a potential loss of privacy.  We will protect your privacy by not collecting your name or identifiable information

There are no expected benefits.

You will be paid $10 for completing the study. Also,additional payment will be made based on your performance in the simulation part of the study. This additional payment will be between $0 and $15. There will be no cost to you to participate in this study.

Your participation in this study is completely voluntary.  You are free not to participate or to withdraw at any time, for whatever reason.  No matter what decision you make, there will be no penalty or loss of benefits to which you are otherwise entitled.

How Will You Keep My Study Records Confidential?

We will make every effort to keep your records confidential. However, there are times when federal or state law requires the disclosure of your records.

The following people or groups may review your study records for purposes such as quality control or safety:

·         The Researcher and any member of their research team

·         The Institutional Review Board at Boston University. The Institutional Review Board is a group of people who review human research studies for safety and protection of people who take part in the studies.

·         The sponsor or funding agency for this study

·         Federal and state agencies that oversee or review research

·Central University Offices
