Thank you for your interest in our study !

In this study, you will pretend to be a manager of a small bakery. Your goal will be to maximize your total profit by deciding how many breads to order from your supplier every day (round) for the next {{ total_rounds }} days (rounds).

This study will help us learn more about the use of information provided by an algorithm in a complex business setting. We expect the study to take around {{ duration }} minutes to complete. You may exit the study at any time for any reason.

Here is the brief overview of the study:

  • Information Sheet
  • Detailed Explanation of the study
  • Demo Rounds ({{ demo_rounds }} Rounds)
  • Simulation ({{ total_rounds }} Rounds)
  • Survey

The decision you make in each round affects how much money you earn. Therefore, please pay attention when you are making decision. You'll be asked to go over the history of your decisions for at least 15 seconds after every 5 rounds. The monetary amounts represented in the study is experimental dollars (denoted by E$). You will also be paid {{ participation_pay }} USD for the completion of the study.

Additionally, the experimental dollars you earn during the simulation will be converted to dollars (USD) at the end of the study with the rate of {{ rate |c }} to 1 USD. In this study, you can earn up performance bonus based on your performance.

There will be multiple tests throughout the study. Please pay attention. Your bonus will be affected by the answer you give to those questions.
