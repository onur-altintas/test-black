This form describes a research study that is being conducted by Onur Altintas, a PhD Candidate from Boston University and his Faculty Advisor, Professor Abraham Seidmann. Onur Altintas can be reached at onura@bu.edu and Professor Abraham Seidmann can be reached at avis@bu.edu.

Purpose:
The purpose of this study is to investigate the effect of decision support systems on decision processes in the newsvendor setting. We are asking you to take part in this study because you have completed at least one statistics course in college.

Duration:
If you decide to take part in this study, you will be asked to make decisions in a simulation that will take about {{ duration }} minutes.

Risks and Benefits:
There is no physical risk in this study. You might get tired during the tasks. You can rest at any time. Some of the survey questions may be upsetting or make you feel uncomfortable. You can skip any of the questions you do not want to answer. There are no expected benefits.

Loss of Confidentiality:
We will not collect and store any directly identifiable information about you with your survey responses. All of the information we collect will be stored on an encrypted hard disk and/or an encrypted cloud drive. Only the members of the research team will have access to it.
