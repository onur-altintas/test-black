It is important to understand how BakerAI works when you decide whether to let BakerAI to order on your behalf or not. Therefore, we would like to spend a couple minutes to explain the dynamics of the A.I. software below.

How BakerAI software works:

If you were sold-out:
Let's assume that in the evening of Day 6, you ordered 100 loaves to sell on Day 7. You sold all of 100 loaves of bread you ordered on Day 7.

Day Amount
ordered		 Amount
sold		 Amount
leftover
7 100 loaves 100 loaves 0 loaves
If you decide to use the A.I. for the next day (for Day 8), the A.I. will order more because you were sold out on Day 7.

If you had some leftovers:
On Day 7, you ordered 100 loaves and you were sold-out. In the evening of Day 7, you decided to order 150 loaves (instead of 100 loaves) for Day 8. On Day 8, you didn't have that much demand for the bread and you could only sell 40 loaves.
Day Amount
ordered		 Amount
sold		 Amount
leftover
8 150 loaves 40 loaves 90 loaves
If you decide to use the A.I. for the next day (Day 9), the A.I. will order less than 150 loaves for Day 9, because you had some leftovers on Day 8.

The exact order quantity amount will be determined by the A.I. software.
